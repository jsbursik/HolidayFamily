THE HOLIDAY LIFETIME WARRANTY

The Holiday Auto Group's new Lifetime Powertrain Warranty guarantees you the peace of mind when it comes to your vehicle purchase - and the best part is: it's completely free. We understand that purchasing a vehicle is not just a transaction; it's an investment in the journeys you'll take and the memories you'll create. Therefore, we wanted to ensure that our customers receive nothing short of the best.

The Lifetime Powertrain Protection, covering both parts and labor for an unlimited number of miles and years, exemplifies our pledge to go above and beyond industry standards. It's more than just a warranty; it's a promise of quality and a commitment to a lifelong partnership with Holiday Auto Group.

WHICH VEHICLES QUALIFY?

When you purchase a new vehicle from any Holiday dealership, it automatically qualifies and even comes with a free first oil change. Any used vehicle under 100,000 miles and made within 10 years of purchase qualifies for Lifetime Powertrain Protection too!

WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS?

In order to help maintain the longevity and health of your vehicle, we only require that all manufacturer recommended service be done at one of the Holiday dealerships.

WE MEAN LIFETIME!

The Holiday Lifetime Powertrain Warranty covers your vehicle for an unlimited number of miles and years as long as you own the vehicle.
